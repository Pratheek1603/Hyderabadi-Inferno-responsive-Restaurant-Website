About Us

Welcome to Hyderabadi Inferno, Nestled in the heart of Hyderabadi, our restaurant has been serving up authentic Biryani and Mughlai delicacies for over a decade. With a passion for traditional Indian cuisine, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting dishes that evoke the rich flavors and aromas of the Mughal Empire.

Our Menu Join us for a culinary journey through the flavors of Hyderabad. Whether you're a seasoned foodie or simply looking for a delicious meal, our restaurant is the perfect destination. Experience the magic of our Biryani and Mughlai delicacies today. we have something for everyone. We also have a full bar with a wide selection of beers, wines, and cocktails.

We are dedicated to providing excellent customer service and making every visit to our restaurant a memorable one. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always happy to help you choose the perfect dish or drink to suit your taste.

Thank you for choosing Hyderabadi Inferno. We look forward to serving you !
